Roast Tainha

Description

These Tainha are healthy and delicious to share with friends. Guests can enjoy the fire to bake onions, potatoes, and pineapples while the fish bakes.It takes 90-120 minutes to prepare and enjoy the roasted fish.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tainhas
  • 2 Onions
  • 8 Potatoes
  • Coarse Salt
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Pineapple
  • Cinnamon
  • Lemon
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Make the fire.
  2. Make cuts in the Tainha and insert coarse salt into it.
  3. Stick the onions, potatoes, and pineapple. Put to bake.
  4. When the fire is ready, place the Tainha into a grill and bake.
  5. After 75 minutes turn the Tainha.
  6. Serve the onions and potatoes with salt and butter.
  7. Another 30 min and serve the Tainha.
  8. Salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Enjoy it!!!
