Crepioca

Description

Tapioca is a versatile food that can be prepared with sweet or savory fillings. It is a source of carbohydrates and provides energy, and can then be consumed for breakfast or intermediate snacks.It takes 5-10 minutes to prepare and enjoy the tapioca.

Ingredients

  • Tapioca
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Salt the tapioca.
  2. Heat a frying pan over low heat and place the tapioca dough, right after dispensing with the aid of a spoon, so that it is homogeneous.
  3. As soon as lightly browned, turn the tapioca and do the same process on the other side.
  4. Add the ham and cheese, fold in half, and sprinkle the oregano. Finally, just serve!
